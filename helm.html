Helm

This page is about helms and masks in the game. Masks are recurring Items in The Legend of Zelda series

There are nine masks in The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild. The first four can be purchased from Kilton the Monster Parts Merchant. The last five require specific amiibo.

Wearing each mask will provide protection from those particular monsters as long as the mask is worn (but only temporary for the Lynel).
